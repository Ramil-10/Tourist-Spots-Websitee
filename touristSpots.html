FEATURED DESTINATION

VIGAN CITY

LOCATION: Vigan is located in the province of Ilocos Sur on the western coast of the island of Luzon in the northern Philippines. It is the capital city of Ilocos Sur and lies along the coast facing the West Philippine Sea.

DESCRIPTION: Vigan is a historic city in northern Philippines and the capital of Ilocos Sur. It is best known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture , making it one of the most important cultural and heritage destinations in the country.

PUERTO PRINCESA UNDER GROUND

LOCATION: Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park is located on the northern coast of Palawan in the western Philippines. It lies about 80 km northwest of Puerto Princesa, within the municipality of Sabang.

DESCRIPTION: The Puerto Princesa Underground River is one of the most famous natural attractions in the Philippines and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

TINUY-AN WATER FALLS

LOCATION: Tinuy-an Falls is located in Barangay Borboanan, Bislig, in the province of Surigao del Sur on the island of Mindanao. It is about 45-60 minutes from Bislig City proper by road.

DESCRIPTION: Tinuy-an Falls is a spectacular multi-tiered waterfall often called the "Little Niagara Falls of the Philippines" because of its wide curtain-like cascade. The falls measure approximately 95 meters wide and 55 meters high, making them one of the widest waterfalls in the Philippines.

SAMAL ISLAND

LOCATION: Samal Island, officially known as the Island Garden City of Samal, is located in the province of Davao del Norte in the southern Philippines. It lies in the Davao Gulf, just off the coast of Davao City, and is accessible by ferry from Davao City.

DESCRIPTION: Samal Island is the largest resort island in the Philippines and is known for its beautiful beaches, clear waters, coral reefs, caves, and nature parks.
